Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Mince
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomate paste
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Lasagne Sheets
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Roast the mince, ad garlic, onion and tomato paste
  2. Cook it for 30 minutes
  3. Melt butter in a small pot, mix the flour with a whisk
  4. Add milk to make it a smooth sauce
  5. Leave it on the cooker for 30 minutes so it looses its taste of flour
  6. Layer the bolognese, lasagne sheets and béchamel sauce. Sprinkle with cheese
  7. Leave it in the oven for 30-40 minutes at 180°C
