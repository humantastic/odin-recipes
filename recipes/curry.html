Curry

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Coconut milk
  • Curry powder
  • Hot Pepper
  • Scallions
  • Vegetables of your choice

Steps

  1. Roast the chicken meat
  2. Cut the scallions and the hot pepper
  3. When the chicken is done, put by the meat and add scallions and hot pepper
  4. Add the coconut milk and let it cook for a few minutes
  5. Add the curry powder
  6. Add the vegetables of your choice and let it all cook together until the vegetables are ready
  7. Serve with side-dishes of your choice (e.g. rice)
