Pasta

Ingredients

  • Pasta of your choice
  • Sieved tomatos
  • Vegetable broth
  • Herbal creme cheese
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Pepper, Salt, Basil and other spices of your choice (e.g. oregano)

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta al dente
  2. Chop the onion and one garlic bulb and lightly saut√© them in a pan
  3. Add 200 ml of sieved tomatos, 100 ml of vegetable broth und 100 g of herbal creme cheese and boil it down to a sauce
  4. Add spices of your choice
  5. Garnish the pasta with some fresh basil on top
